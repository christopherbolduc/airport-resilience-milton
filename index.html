Hurricane Milton: Airport Resilience Analysis (Oct 2024)

Comparing recovery performance of Florida airports post-hurricane

Executive Summary

This project evaluates the operational resilience of five Florida airports affected by Hurricane Milton. Using flight and weather data, I developed a resilience score to assess recovery performance relative to storm severity. DAB performed best; SRQ showed the longest delays.

Scoring Formula

    ((peak_wind_speed_kmh ** 0.5) / 10)
